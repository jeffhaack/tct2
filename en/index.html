What is the
Transcaucasian Trail?

The Transcaucasian Trail, or TCT, is the greatest hiking route you’ve never heard of. The Caucasus, on the border of Europe and Asia, is one of the most biologically and culturally diverse mountain ranges in the world, but one that few have had the chance to explore. Now our growing community of hikers, cartographers and conservationists is putting this hidden gem on the map to help ensure that the natural and cultural heritage of the region can be enjoyed by future generations. Join our community and help us create a truly world-class hiking route in one of the most intriguing regions on Earth.

Learn about the TCT

A mountain trail covering thousands of kilometers of 3000+ meter passes and valleys filled with ancient cultures and endemic species, across 17 distinct regions where over a dozen different languages are spoken. Sound exciting? It is.


Who are we?

We are a community of international and local hikers, explorers, conservationists, photographers, cartographers, travelers and supporters passionate about the people and ecosystems of the Caucasus, even if some of us have not been there… yet.


Get Involved

Behind every great long-distance trail is a community of volunteers that make a line drawn on a map into a hiking trail that can be enjoyed by thousands of people. We are helping to build and organize that community. Join us in making the Transcaucasian Trail a reality.
